About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and values

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower students by keeping them connected to their campus life. We aim to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience that ensures you never miss out on any opportunity to engage, learn, or have fun.

Our Vision

Event Connect is more than just a calendar; it’s a dynamic platform designed with students in mind. Whether it’s a workshop, seminar, sports event, or social gathering, our system updates in real-time to provide you with the latest information. With just a few clicks, you can explore a wide range of events, add them to your personal schedule, and even receive reminders so you’re always in the loop.
