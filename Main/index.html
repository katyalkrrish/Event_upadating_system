About Us


"Event Connect" is an innovative event updating system designed specifically for college students. This system aims to provide students with a platform where they can easily stay informed about various events happening on campus or in the surrounding community. By using Event Connect, students can discover a diverse range of events, such as social gatherings, academic seminars, volunteer opportunities, and more.

Event Connect offers personalized event recommendations based on the user's interests, allowing students to explore new activities and connect with like-minded individuals. Additionally, the system provides timely updates and reminders for upcoming events, ensuring that students never miss out on any exciting opportunities.

With Event Connect, college students can make the most of their university experience by staying engaged, networking with peers, and enriching their personal and academic growth through participation in a wide array of events.
